# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 26, 2026

Scanly ("the app") is developed by Jeevaprakash G. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Summary

Scanly is an offline-first document scanner. Your scans, documents, and preferences are stored on your device. Scanly does not require an account, does not sell personal data, and does not include advertising or third-party analytics.

## Information stored on your device

When you use Scanly, the app stores the following locally on your phone or tablet:

- scanned page images and generated PDF exports you create
- document titles, folders/collections, and page order
- app preferences such as theme mode and camera display options
- temporary processing and export cache files

This data remains on your device unless you delete it in the app, remove the app, or clear app storage from Android settings.

## Permissions

Scanly may request access to:

- **Camera** — to capture document pages
- **Photos / media** — when you import images from your gallery
- **Internet** — only for optional features such as checking for app updates or opening links you choose in Settings

Scanly does not access your camera, files, or network except when you use the related feature.

## Optional network use

If you choose to check for updates, Scanly contacts GitHub to read public release information for this app. No document content is uploaded during an update check.

If you tap external links in Settings (for example, the project website, portfolio, or support email), your browser or email app handles that request outside Scanly.

## Data deletion

You can delete Scanly's library data at any time from **Settings → Clear all data**. Uninstalling the app removes app-private storage. Files saved outside the app through export or share actions are not removed automatically.

## Children's privacy

Scanly is a general productivity app and is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Scanly's features or legal requirements change. The "Last updated" date above will be revised when that happens.
